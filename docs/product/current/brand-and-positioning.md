# Brand And Positioning

## About JustSow

JustSow is inspired by Jesus' teaching in Matthew 13:1-9, where a sower scatters seed widely, trusting God to bring growth. The platform exists to help generous sowing of money and encouragement support creative evangelists sharing the gospel.

## Purpose

JustSow funds bold and innovative evangelism projects that may otherwise struggle to get started. By providing seed funding, the platform helps individuals and teams move from vision to action and multiply their impact.

## Funding Model

- initial seed grants support new or early-stage projects
- selected projects may later receive further support
- the platform exists to fund multiple projects rather than concentrate on a single initiative

## Vision

The long-term vision is to see the gospel proclaimed boldly across nations by helping creative evangelists launch and grow faithful, effective projects.
